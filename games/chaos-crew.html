CLUSTER PAYS

Groups of 4+ matching adjacent symbols (horizontal/vertical) form winning clusters. Wilds substitute for any symbol except scatter.

Payouts scale with cluster size:
4-5 symbols: 25% of multiplier
6-7 symbols: 50%
8-9 symbols: 75%
10+ symbols: 100%

TUMBLE

After a win, winning symbols are removed and new symbols fall in. A multiplier increases by x1 with each consecutive tumble.

HOLD & SPIN BONUS

Land 3+ 💀 scatters to trigger Hold & Spin. Each reel has its own multiplier. Land symbols to increase multipliers. Final win = bet x sum of all reel multipliers.

Bonus symbols:
🇷🇺 Adder: adds to that reel multiplier
🇷🇺✨ Global Adder: adds to ALL reel multipliers
🇺🇸 Multiplier: multiplies that reel multiplier
🇺🇸✨ Global Multiplier: multiplies ALL reel multipliers
